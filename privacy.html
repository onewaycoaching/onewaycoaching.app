Overview

OneWay is a calorie and energy balance tracking app for iPhone. Version 1.0 keeps your data on your device. We do not operate user accounts, cloud servers, or analytics in v1.

Information we collect

Data you enter — Calorie logs, notes, and settings are stored locally on your iPhone (SwiftData). We do not receive copies.

Apple Health (HealthKit) — With your permission, OneWay: Deficit reads body weight, active energy burned, and resting energy burned to show your daily balance. Version 1.0 does not write to HealthKit. Revoke access anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → OneWay: Deficit.

We do not collect — Names, emails, location, contacts, photos, advertising IDs, or third-party analytics in v1.

How we use information

All processing happens on your device. We do not sell or share personal information.

Data retention

Data remains on your device until you delete the app or reset in-app data. Uninstalling OneWay removes locally stored app data subject to iOS behavior.

Children

OneWay is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. We will post the new version at this URL and update the “Last updated” date.

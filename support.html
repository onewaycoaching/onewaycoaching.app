Correction factor recommendations

After enough logged days and weigh-ins, OneWay may suggest a correction factor based on your data. Recommendations are computed locally on your iPhone from your logged calories, Apple Health burn, and weight trend. They are not medical advice and may not reflect short-term water weight changes. You choose whether to apply a suggestion in Settings.

Common topics

  • Weight in OneWay — Sync a smart scale to Apple Health, or log weight in the Health app. Chain: scale → Health → OneWay.
  • Burn in OneWay — Apple Watch writes active and resting calories to Health; OneWay reads the combined daily total.
  • HealthKit permissions — Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → OneWay
  • Data on device — OneWay v1 stores logs locally; uninstalling removes app data
